About 5th Kyu

5th Kyu (Green Belt) will typically be the second test you take, anywhere from six months to one year into practice, 5th kyu includes all previous Ukemi/Taisabaki as well as a new kata, Kihon Kata I, plus two new throws, Sasaetsurikomi Ashi and Hiza Guruma

Exam Material

  1. Ukemi (Falling Techniques) - Ushiro, Migi, Hidari, Mae Mawari
  2. Taisabaki - Stepping, hand sets, kicking sets, twisting, striking
  3. Kata (Sequences) - Kihon Kata I
  4. Nage Waza (Throwing Techniques) - Sasaetsurikomi Ashi, Hiza Guruma
