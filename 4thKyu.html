About 4th Kyu

4th Kyu (Green Belt, Brown Stripe) will typically be the third test you take, anywhere from 12 months to 18 months into practice, 4th kyu includes all previous Ukemi/Taisabaki as well as a new kata, Goshin Ho II, as well as some Jiyu Waza

Exam Material

  1. Ukemi (Falling Techniques) - Ushiro, Migi, Hidari, Mae Mawari
  2. Taisabaki - Stepping, hand sets, kicking sets, twisting, striking
  3. Kata (Sequences) - Goshin Ho II
  4. Jiyu Waza - strikes only, prescribed counters
