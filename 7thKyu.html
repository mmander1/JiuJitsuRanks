About 7th Kyu

-7th Kyu (white belt green stripe) is the first rank you’ll test for in Nihon Jiujitsu, often times since testing is only every 6 months 7th Kyu and 6th Kyu will be combined into one test, so you’ll progress from white belt to a green belt with a white stripe.

Exam Material

  1. Ukemi (Falling Techniques) - Ushiro, Migo, Hidari, Mae Mawari
  2. Taisabaki - Stepping, hand sets, kicking sets, twisting, striking
  3. Nage Waza (Throwing Techniques) - Seoi Nage, Osoto Gari
