About 6th Kyu

6th Kyu (Green Belt, White Stripe) will typically be the first test you take, combining 7th and 6th kyu tests. 6th Kyu includes Goshin Ho I, which is the first kata we learn, as well as a new throw, Ukigoshi

Exam Material

  1. Ukemi (Falling Techniques) - Ushiro, Migi, Hidari, Mae Mawari
  2. Taisabaki - Stepping, hand sets, kicking sets, twisting, striking
  3. Kata (Sequences) - Goshin Ho I 
  4. Nage Waza (Throwing Techniques) - Seoi Nage, Osoto Gari, Ukigoshi
