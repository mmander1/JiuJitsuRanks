About Shodan

Shodan (Black Belt) is considered the gateway to advanced jujitsu, it includes executing Kihon Kata II at a high competency as well as Jiyu Waza from multiple attackers.

Exam Material

  1. Ukemi (Falling Techniques) - Ushiro, Migi, Hidari, Mae Mawari Tobu Ukemi (flying fall)
  2. Taisabaki - Stepping, hand sets, kicking sets, twisting, striking advanced stepping, striking, and blocking
  3. Kata (Sequences) - Kihon Kata II (at a high competency)
  4. Nage Waza - Taiotoshi, Tomoe Nage
  5. Jiyu Waza - multiple attackers, prescribed counters
