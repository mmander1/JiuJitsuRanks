About 3rd Kyu

3rd Kyu (Brown Belt, Green Stripe) will typically be the fourth test you take, usually around two years into practice. 4th kyu includes all previous Ukemi/Taisabaki but also adds advanced stepping, striking and blocking techniques. You also repeat Kihon Kata I at a higher level and perform two new throws, Ouchi Gari and Kouchi Gari. Last but not least we add some new Jiyu Waza with some added strikes and wrist grabs.

Exam Material

  1. Ukemi (Falling Techniques) - Ushiro, Migi, Hidari, Mae Mawari, Tobu Ukemi (flying fall)
  2. Taisabaki - Stepping, hand sets, kicking sets, twisting, striking advanced stepping, striking, and blocking
  3. Kata (Sequences) - Kihon Kata I (at a higher level)
  4. Nage Waza - Ouchi Gari, Kouchi Gari
  5. Jiyu Waza - strikes and wrist grabs, prescribed counters
